Question 1 - Main Differences between Web1, Web2 and Web3

  1. Web1 considered as the "read-only" web, Web2 considered as the "read-write" web and Web3 is kind of "read-write-execute" web. This principle expresses the user's growing interaction with the web according to it's different versions.
  2. The popularity grew dramaticly over the years - from Million of users (Web1), through Billion of users (Web2) and Trillion of users (Web3).
  3. Web1 main goal was connect information, Web2 succeeded to connect people and Web3 connect knowledge (Web of DATA). An example of it's ability to connect people is the establishment of blloging and social network that started in Web2 (Facebook, Twiter).In addition, the collaborative knowledge approach and media sharing reflected through Wikipedia, StackOverFlow and Youtube.
  4. ################ Web3 is also known as the Semantic Web - using AI tools the web learns the user and provides him relevant informatiom and personalaized experience.
